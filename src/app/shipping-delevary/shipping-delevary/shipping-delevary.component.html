1 Introduction

At FashionMaster4u, we are committed to delivering your orders quickly and safely. This Shipping & Delivery Policy outlines our delivery timelines, shipping methods, and related information to ensure a smooth shopping experience.

Pan-India Delivery

We deliver to all major cities and towns across India. Our logistics network ensures your orders reach you safely and on time.

2 Delivery Timeline

We understand the importance of timely delivery. Here's our delivery schedule:

Normal Orders 10–15 Days

Standard orders are processed and delivered within 10–15 days from the date of order confirmation.

Bulk Orders 10–15 Business Days

Bulk orders (typically 10+ items or custom orders) are delivered within 10–15 business days. Additional time may be required for customization.

3 Shipping Methods

We partner with trusted logistics providers to ensure safe and timely delivery:

  • Standard Shipping: Regular delivery service covering all major cities and towns
  • Express Shipping: Available for select locations with faster delivery (additional charges may apply)
  • Cash on Delivery (COD): Available for most locations — pay when you receive your order
  • Secure Packaging: All items are carefully packaged to prevent damage during transit

5 Order Tracking

Stay updated on your order status with our tracking system:

  • Tracking Number: You'll receive a tracking number via email/SMS once your order is dispatched
  • Real-Time Updates: Track your order status in real-time through your account or using the tracking link
  • Delivery Notifications: Receive SMS/email notifications at each stage of delivery

6 Shipping Charges

Our shipping charges are transparent and competitive:

  • Free Shipping: Available on orders above a certain value — check current offers at checkout
  • Standard Charges: Shipping charges vary based on location, weight, and order value. Charges are displayed at checkout before payment
  • COD Charges: Additional charges may apply for Cash on Delivery orders

7 Delivery Locations

We deliver to locations across India:

  • Metro Cities: Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, Kolkata, Hyderabad, Pune, and other major cities
  • Tier 2 & 3 Cities: We deliver to most tier 2 and tier 3 cities across India
  • Remote Areas: Delivery to remote areas may take additional time. Please check serviceability at checkout

Important: Please ensure your delivery address is complete and accurate. Incorrect addresses may lead to delivery delays or failed deliveries. We are not responsible for delays caused by incorrect address information.

8 Delivery Instructions

To ensure smooth delivery, please keep the following in mind:

  • Available Recipient: Please ensure someone is available at the delivery address during business hours to receive the order
  • Contact Number: Keep your registered mobile number active and accessible for delivery coordination
  • Inspection: Please inspect the package before accepting delivery. Report any damage immediately
  • Failed Delivery: If delivery fails due to unavailability, we'll attempt delivery again. Multiple failed attempts may result in order cancellation
